About this blog

October 7, 2013

You may also try to:

  • Learn about or contact the Author
  • Inform yourself about the legal stuff

In 2013, I decided that I would write up some pieces about the books I read. At first I planned to put them on Google+. But I was not very happy about what could be done there in terms of formatting, overview or references to other reviews. So the decision came to start this blog.

But a lot has happened since then. Google+ ended on the Google graveyard and I decided to move my blog away from Blogger due to that. It took me a while, but eventually I arrived here.

But some things have not changed: I will use the blog to happily provide my point of view on books, games, movies, some gadgets and the occasional other stuff. Criticism of my criticism is welcome.

Don’t expect the blog to be real-time and show reviews when or before a book is officially released. I’ll do the thing (reading, blogging) when I feel like it. Be aware that a lot of my binge reading actually happens on vacation or during long travels.

Some may wonder why I write in English. As a native German speaker living and working in Germany, this does not seem an intuitive choice. But:

  • About 99% of the books I read are originally written in English. Translation is usually something a book does not profit from. Then there is the aspect of time. When I read books, most are not yet translated (which caused the switch when I was hooked up on David Webers Harrington series) and some may never will. So if I read them in English, I should criticize them in that language as well.
  • Also a significant part of my circles on social media are non-Germans and English is the current lingua franca.
  • And not the least, just because I want to. It find it quite some challenge. While a significant portion of my conversations on the job are in English, I rarely write more than a few lines there.

P.S. I will update this blog article from time to time. While other entries will mostly receive corrections only, this one hopefully will be extended over time.

On Comments

Currently the Blog does not support comments. During the seven years on Blogger (with comments) I attracted about a dozen or so non-Spam comments. Therefore those are currently not high on my priority list. If you need to get something of your chest and into my ear, please write me an email.
